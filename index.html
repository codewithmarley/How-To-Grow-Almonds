HARVESTING

Harvesting Almonds Is Pretty Easy Just Pick Them When They Are Loose And Pink/Red, Some Breeds Will Start To Open Others Will Stay Closed,
Usualy Just Shaking A Branch Will Make Them Fall

Seperating The Good And Bad

After Harvesting, Seperate The Good And Bad...
Here Are Some Tips On How To Check Which To Keep And Which to Throw Away

Good

  • Purple/Red/Orange/Brown
  • No Holes
  • No Bites
  • etc.

Bad

  • Green, Yellow, Black
  • Holes
  • Worms
  • etc.

Drying

You Will Want To Make Sure They Get Sun But Also Dont Get Wet!
They Usualy Get Brown And Harder When They Are Dry
You Can Also Eat The Fruit Before It Dries Or Peel It Off And Blend It Into A Juice Or Ice-cream

Cracking

When They Are Done Drying They Can Be Cracked After The Fruit Is Eaten/Peeled With A Nut Cracker
Or You Can Just Pound Them With A Rock One At A Time Without Having To Peel It
